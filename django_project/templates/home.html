About Dog Or Cat:

Welcome to "Dog Or Cat". This is a guessing game that you have to distinguish wether in a blured image is depicted a Dog or a Cat.


- Single Player

You have 3 lives in total and you lose one each time you make a wrong guess. For each correct answer you get 100 points. The goal is to get as many points as you can to reach the top of the leaderboard.


- Time Attack

Clock is ticking! You have 1 minute to answer as many questions as you can! For each correct answer you get 100 points. If you can guess right 3 pictures in a row you are in a Streak and now each correct answer rewards you with 300 points! Making a wrong guess will put an end to your Streak. Until your next one of course.


- Multiplayer (Coming soon)

Challenge your friends in a batch of 12 pictures. You play first and your score out of 12 is the one they have to beat. Both got the same score? The one who did it faster gets the W!
